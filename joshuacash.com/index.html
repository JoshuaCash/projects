About Me

I have searched most of my life to find a career that is challenging. Not that I'm super smart but I have a natural curiosity and obsession with finding out how things work. Even what seems mundane to some, I want to know why it is and how it works. I have taken jobs purely for knowledge and once I feel that I have satisfied my hunger I lose interest and move on.
Well, I'm almost positive I have met my match.... Coding, programming, designing and tech in general are ever changing and evolving at speeds that I could only dream of keeping up with. There is always a new problem to solve and a better, faster way to do it.
Figuring it out is my passion.
